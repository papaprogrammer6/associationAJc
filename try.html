Logo & Name Radio Web Challenge

Introduction

Welcome to the Logo & Name Radio Web Challenge! This is your chance to showcase your design skills and creativity by developing a logo and name for our new web radio. We are looking for innovative and original designs that capture the essence of integration, social cohesion, and youth themes.

Challenge Details

Logo Requirements

  • Colors: The logo must include the colors with the HEX codes #a3bb1b (green) and #ef7c09 (orange).

    • #a3bb1b

    #ef7c09
  • Theme: The logo should be compatible with integration and social cohesion.
  • Design: It should feature a nice shape and color, reflecting creativity and originality.

Name Requirements

  • Attributes: The name should be brief and attractive.
  • Compatibility: It should match the logo and be suitable for the surroundings.
  • Theme: The name should reflect integrative integration and youth themes.

Rewards

  • Prizes: Exciting prizes for the best designs.
